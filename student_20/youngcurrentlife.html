  • Young Billie

    Billie is born and raised in Los Angeles. She is a sagittarius born on December 18, 2001. She lived in a family of musicians and actors which as we can tell did impact her. She is the doughter of actress and screenwriter Maggie Baird and actor Patrick O'Connell who are both musicians. She was home-schooled with her brother Finneas. At the age of eight, she joined the Los Angeles Childrens Choir. Her mother tought the two basic songwriting and Billie wrote her first song at 11 for Maggie's songwriting class. The song was inspired by the series The Walking Dead. Billie also went to some acting auditions which she didn't like.

  • Current Billie

    Billie is still living in her childhood home with her parents. She still creates music with her brother Finneas in his small room. Billie is starting her tour "Where Do We Go" on March 9 of 2020. She also uploaded her latest song "No Time to Die" which became an amazing hit and is going to be the main song of the upcoming movie, "No Time To Die"
